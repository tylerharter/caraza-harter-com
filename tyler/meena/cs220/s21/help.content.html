How can I get help ?

To help you succeed we have assembled a team of 14 TAs and 16 peer-mentors. They will be available to help you throughout the course for answering your questions, addressing your concerns.

We will be offering the following avenues of communication for you:
  • Online synchronous meeting spaces
    • Lab Hours
    • Office hours
    • Social hours
  • Online asynchronous forums
    • Piazza (Recommended)
    • Email (*only for emergencies)

Lab Hours

Lab hours will be led by a TA / peer mentor. All students are expected to complete the weekly lab before attempting to solve the corresponding project. Our recommendation is that you should either go through the lab on your own before the lab timing or attend the synchronous lab session. Link to lab sessions can be found on Canvas->BBCollaborate Ultra. Please make sure to select the session corresponding to the lecture that you are enrolled in (example: Lab Hours: section 1).

Office Hours

One-on-One sessions where TAs, peer-mentors, and instructors will be present to answer your questions. This space is for all kinds of queries, especially those related to debugging your programs. TAs and peer-mentors will be present in separate breakout rooms and all conversations with you will be private.

  • The availability of TAs and peer-mentors is posted Piazza->Office Hours. The schedule will be updated on a weekly basis and is subject to change!

  • To allow for us to help you in a meaningful manner please submit a ticket below before joining the office hours

Please complete the following steps to submit your request:

  • Step 1: Please use the following form to submit a request to be assigned a TA/Peer-mentor to talk to. Please try incognito window of Chrome browser if link doesn't work!

  • Step 2: Track your position in the queue. Once your ticket has be submitted you can track your position in the queue via this dashboard.

If you are not already waiting in the main room, you will have about 2-5 Mins from that point onwards to join the meeting via Canvas->BBCollaborate Ultra.

Social Hours

Session where you can meet with other students in the class. It is an open to all session. This space is for you to meet other students in the class and have conversations. All campus rules are applicable to this social session!. You can access the Social Hours using Canvas->BBCollaborate Ultra.

Piazza

Piazza is an online forum for asking and answering questions about course material. You can access the course piazza page via Tools->Piazza. Piazza sign-up counts for grade points. Please read syllabus page for further details. Please read our expectations of you when using piazza. Please see this guide before making a post on Piazza.

Email

We prefer you to use Office hours to resolve your debugging issues. To contact us over email, send an email to your assigned TA (Resources->Who is my TA?).

Subject Tags

Your email subject line needs to have the following format. This will help us respond back to you in more effective and prompt manner.

[ Regrade | Admin | Exam | Quiz | Project] [Quiz # | Exam #| Project # | Topic Name | lesson #] 5-6 word summary of the query

Lets say you wanted help related to the exam, then your email would look as follows: 

  To: assigned_ta@wisc.edu; cs220-fall20-help@cs.wisc.edu
  Sub: [Exam] [Exam-1] Can't access exam solutions
  Hi TA,
      I need help with ...
